Manage Schedules

Schedules are used to specify when a Service is offered by a Resource.

How you define a Schedule is no different from other types of schedules you may have encountered: they start on a date, at a particular time, may end on a date, or possibly repeat indefinitely, and end at a particular time. They can fall on particular days of the week and repeat daily, weekly, monthly, yearly even. You can also specify that a Schedule is available the first Monday of the month, or every other Friday, etc.

When modifying Schedules, such as adding only one available weekend a month to an existing weekday schedule, you will work with TimeWindows. TimeWindows are just small pieces that together make up the entire availability of a Schedule. In fact when first creating a Schedule, a TimeWindow is automatically created for you.
